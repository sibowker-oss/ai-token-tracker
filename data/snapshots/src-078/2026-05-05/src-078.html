Data (NEM)

Accurate, up-to-date data is vital for the day-to-day management of the National Electricity Market (NEM). This section of the site provides data, numbers and figures relating to the operation of the NEM, including data about ancillary services, metering, networks and settlements. It also includes data feeds from the NEM’s Market Management System (MMS).

If you can’t find the information you’re looking for, other NEM data sets, as well as reports and analysis, are published elsewhere on the site. Key locations include NEM forecasting and planning, NEM events and reports, and Major publications.

Data dashboard

An at-a-glance view of the NEM’s key data points, including price and demand, dispatch, and short and medium-term outlooks.

Generation and load

Actual generation data for each generation unit, as well as the actual load for each scheduled load.
