The Firm

Hepburn Advisory is an independent advisory firm focused on the economics of the AI industry. We work with founders, investors, and enterprise leaders who need defensible numbers and operator-grade thinking on the AI stack.

Our flagship publication, The AI Ledger, triangulates primary sources — 10-K filings, earnings calls, API telemetry, and industry disclosures — into three interlocking views of the AI economy: capital, revenue, and usage. We publish without editorial lean so the data can be cited across the hype/skeptic spectrum.

Alongside the Ledger we advise AI-native founders on go-to-market and enterprise IT leaders on AI adoption and vendor selection.
