The Layer-Stack Multiplier

For every $1 of customer Apps Revenue, how many dollars flow down the stack to Compute, Silicon, and Power. The recycling chart nobody else publishes.

AI Ledger · Hepburn Advisory
Read this chart this way: Apps Revenue is what end customers pay (model-lab APIs, Apps SaaS, AI-app subscriptions). Compute is what cloud providers earn — net of model-lab pass-through (per D6). Silicon is what NVIDIA + AMD + custom ASIC vendors earn from compute providers' capex. Power is what utilities + DC infra vendors earn. The asymmetry between Compute and Silicon (silicon revenue exceeds compute revenue on a single-year basis) is the “circular financing” story — silicon spend is funded by capex, not revenue passes-through.
