The Protest Risk

US DC sites facing community protest~40%
Capacity at risk~7 GW
Revenue at risk (7GW x $10B/GW)$70B/yr
Data centre construction is meeting significant community resistance across the US. Water usage, power grid strain, and noise are the primary concerns. An estimated 40% of protested projects face cancellation or significant delay. At $10B revenue per GW, this represents $70B in annual revenue capacity at risk.
Source: Chamath (speculative estimate)
