Follow the Dollar

Where $1 of "AI revenue" actually goes — from buyer to tokens. Hover over any flow to see the details.

The Shelfware Leak

~$10-12B

Of the ~$38B total AI revenue, roughly $10-12B is "booked AI revenue" that generates minimal actual token consumption. M365 Copilot, Agentforce, and NOW Assist account for the bulk. This money pays for licenses, not intelligence.

The Pure Signal

~$8-10B

AI Natives (Cursor, Perplexity, Harvey, etc.) and direct API consumers convert nearly 100% of revenue into tokens. Every dollar = actual AI work being done. This is the real demand signal.

The Consumer Question

~$14B

Consumer subscriptions (ChatGPT Plus, Claude Pro) are real revenue but discretionary. ~50% converts to tokens (rest is margin, free-tier subsidy). High churn risk — will users keep paying $20/mo as free alternatives improve?
