Shelfware Alert Mar 21, 2026
M365 Copilot: 15M Seats Sold, 35.8% Actually Used
New data from Gartner and enterprise surveys shows Microsoft 365 Copilot at 15M licensed seats ($5.4B annualized) but only 35.8% active usage. Microsoft has been auto-installing Copilot since October 2025 to inflate adoption numbers. UBS analyst Karl Keirstead: "M365 revenue growth is not accelerating due to Copilot." Multiple reports indicate Microsoft's own employees prefer ChatGPT over Copilot.
Dashboard impact: M365 Copilot shelfware rate updated to ~65%. Enterprise SaaS token conversion estimate stays at 10-20%.

This is one of the first examples of frontier AI models directly embedded in financial transaction flows — each payment triggers Claude inference. India is now Anthropic's second-largest market for claude.ai traffic.
Dashboard impact: APAC token volume grows. Anthropic's regional weight for Asia-Pacific increases. Financial Services industry share grows.
